SHORT DESCRIPTION (72 chars):
Open-source crypto wallet for Solana and Bitcoin. Your keys, your coins.

FULL DESCRIPTION:
Salmon Wallet is a community-owned, open-source crypto wallet built for real decentralization. If you can't verify it, you don't own it.

KEY FEATURES
• Multi-chain support: Solana and Bitcoin
• Swap & Bridge: Exchange and transfer tokens across networks seamlessly
• Wallet Cleanup: Remove unwanted tokens and reduce clutter
• dApp Integration: Connect directly to decentralized applications
• Scalable Design: Starts simple, grows more powerful as your needs increase

SECURITY FIRST
• Private keys are encrypted locally on your device
• Password-protected, never transmitted or accessible to anyone
• No tracking or data collection by default
• Transparent security model with fully public source code
• All contributions undergo rigorous inspection before integration

SUPPORTED NETWORKS
• Solana (Mainnet & Devnet)
• Bitcoin (Mainnet & Testnet)

Open code. Open ownership. Salmon Wallet — built for people who believe transparency is not optional.
